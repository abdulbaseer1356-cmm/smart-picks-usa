We buy what we test

Smart Picks USA started with one rule in 2021: never review a product we didn't pay for ourselves. That rule hasn't changed.

Why we exist

Most "best of" lists are ad copy

We got tired of round-ups written from a spec sheet and a press release. So we built a small team that actually lives with the products before scoring them.

01

We buy at retail

No loaner units factor into a Pick Score. If a brand sends one, we test it, but we buy our own for the number that gets published.

02

Same checklist, every time

Each category has a fixed set of tests, so a score from January and a score from June mean the same thing.

03

Commissions don't move scores

Our editorial team scores products before affiliate links are ever added to a review.
